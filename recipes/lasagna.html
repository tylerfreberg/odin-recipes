Lasagna

ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • sauce
  • meat
  • cheese
  1. Place noodles in a baking dish.
  2. Place cheese on noodles.
  3. Place sauce on cheese.
  4. Place meat on sauce.
  5. Repeat the above steps until you place your last noodle.
  6. Bake until done.
  7. Slice, serve, and enjoy.
