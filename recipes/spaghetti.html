Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • sauce
  • parmesan cheese
  1. Boil noodles until al dente.
  2. Drain pasta water.
  3. Turn heat to medium-low and pour sauce into the pot with spaghetti noodles.
  4. Stir until all spaghetti is coated.
  5. Plate spaghetti, sprinkling parmesan cheese on top.
